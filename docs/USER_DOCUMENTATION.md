## Feature Guide

### Feature 1: Ask Questions (Chat)

**What it does:** Get instant answers to permit questions.

#### How to use:

1. **Navigate to "Ask Questions" tab** (first tab)
2. **Type your question** in the chat box at the bottom
3. **Press Enter** or click send
4. **Read the response** from the AI assistant
5. **Ask follow-up questions** for clarification

#### Example Questions:

**Basic Questions:**
- "What permits do I need to build a fence?"
- "How much does a building permit cost?"
- "How long does permit approval take?"

**Specific Questions:**
- "Can I install solar panels without a permit?"
- "Do I need a permit to remodel my bathroom?"
- "What's required for a garage conversion?"

**Complex Questions:**
- "I want to build a 300 sq ft ADU. What permits do I need and how long will it take?"
- "What are the setback requirements for a shed in North Park?"

#### Tips:

✅ **Be specific** - Include project details (size, location, type)
✅ **Ask follow-ups** - The bot remembers your conversation
✅ **Use simple language** - No need for technical jargon

❌ **Don't ask about** - Personal permit status, appeals, specific case numbers

---

### Feature 2: Permit Checklist Generator

**What it does:** Creates a customized permit checklist for your project.

#### How to use:

1. **Navigate to "Permit Checklist" tab** (second tab)
2. **Read the example projects** for inspiration
3. **Describe your project** in one sentence in the text box
4. **Click "Generate Checklist"** button
5. **Review your checklist** with permits, timeline, and costs
6. **Download** as a Markdown file (optional)

#### Example Project Descriptions:

**Good Examples:**
- ✅ "Build a 12x20 wooden deck with stairs and electrical lighting"
- ✅ "Kitchen remodel with new cabinets, countertops, and electrical outlets"
- ✅ "Convert garage to living space with insulation, drywall, and HVAC"

**Bad Examples:**
- ❌ "Do something to my house" (too vague)
- ❌ "Major renovation project with lots of changes" (too general)
- ❌ "Build stuff" (not enough detail)

#### What you'll get:

Your checklist includes:
- **Required Permits** - Specific permits needed
- **Estimated Timeline** - How long each step takes
- **Approximate Costs** - Permit fees and estimates
- **Key Considerations** - Important things to know
- **Next Steps** - What to do first

#### Sample Output:

```markdown
## Permit Checklist: Kitchen Remodel

### Required Permits
1. Building Permit - $300-500
2. Electrical Permit - $150-250
3. Plumbing Permit - $100-200 (if moving fixtures)

### Timeline
- Permit Application: 1-2 weeks to prepare
- Plan Review: 2-4 weeks
- Permit Issuance: 1 week
- Total: 4-7 weeks before starting work

### Key Considerations
- Hire licensed contractors
- Must pass inspections
- Keep permits posted at site
- Final inspection required

### Next Steps
1. Create detailed plans
2. Get contractor quotes
3. Submit permit application
4. Schedule inspections
```

## Tips & Best Practices

### Getting Better Answers

#### ✅ DO:

**Be Specific:**
- ❌ "Tell me about permits"
- ✅ "What permits do I need for a 10x12 shed?"

**Include Context:**
- ❌ "Can I build a fence?"
- ✅ "Can I build a 6-foot wooden fence in my front yard in Pacific Beach?"

**Ask Follow-ups:**
- Start broad, then ask for details
- "What's the timeline for that?"
- "How much will those permits cost?"

**Use Real Examples:**
- Describe your actual project
- Include dimensions, materials, location

#### ❌ DON'T:

**Don't Be Vague:**
- "Do I need permits?" (for what?)
- "How much does it cost?" (what permit?)

**Don't Ask Personal Questions:**
- "What's wrong with my application?"
- "Why was my permit denied?"

**Don't Expect Legal Advice:**
- This tool provides information, not legal counsel
- Complex cases need professional consultation

---

### Checklist Best Practices

#### Write Good Descriptions:

**Template:**
"[Action] a [size] [type] with [features]"

**Examples:**
- "Build a 200 sq ft detached storage shed with electricity"
- "Remodel kitchen with new electrical, plumbing, and gas connections"
- "Install 6kW solar panel system on roof with battery storage"

**Include:**
- Size/dimensions
- Type of work
- Utilities involved (electric, plumbing, gas)
- Location (if relevant)

**Avoid:**
- Unnecessary details about materials
- Budget constraints
- Personal preferences
- Unrelated projects in one description

---

### Meeting Summary Tips

#### Get Better Summaries:

**Choose the Right Neighborhood:**
- Use the dropdown (it's searchable!)
- Type first few letters to find it quickly
- Make sure it's spelled correctly

**Paste Complete Content:**
- Include full transcript or agenda
- Don't edit out sections
- More context = better summary

**What Works Best:**
- Official meeting minutes
- Transcripts
- Detailed agendas
- News articles about meetings

**What Doesn't Work:**
- Personal notes
- Single sentences
- Partial information
- Unrelated content

## Glossary

**ADU** - Accessory Dwelling Unit (granny flat, in-law unit)

**Building Permit** - Permission to construct or modify structures

**Development Services** - City department handling permits

**Municipal Code** - Local laws and regulations

**Setback** - Required distance from property line

**Zoning** - Land use regulations for areas

**Community Planning District** - Geographic planning area (61 in San Diego)

**Council District** - Political representation area (9 in San Diego)
